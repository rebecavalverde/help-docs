They are different kind of activities and assignments you can create in Flat for Education. In order to create your first one you have to make sure that you already [created your first class.](en/education/create-new-class.html)

![Stream view](/help/assets/img/activities/stream.png)

You can open a list of all your existing assignments by clicking on the left icon.
You can start creating a new assignment by pressing the other button on the right

## 1. Pick your assignment type

There are 4 different kind of assignment or activities that you can create:

![Stream view](/help/assets/img/activities/assignmentstype.png)

### New score

Your students will have to create a new score, work on it following your guidelines and submit it back to you.

### Read

This mode is meant to share a score part of your own library without the students being able to edit / alter it.

### Write

## 2. Setup the assignment

### Assignment main information

![Assignment information](/help/assets/img/activities/assignment-title.png)

You will be able to write a name & a description. These information will be accessible from the student assignment at any time while they work on a score.

### Pick the score

![Pick the score](/help/assets/img/activities/pickscore.png)

You will able to browse you score and pick one of them.

ðŸ’¡ We will soon make them searchable.

You should notice that a list be displayed on the right of the score name. It's directly related to the type of assignment you want to create:

![Pick the score - Tips](/help/assets/img/activities/pickscore-tips.png)

* Student can read -> **Read**
* Student can write -> **Write**
* Student will get a copy -> **Template**
* No score selected -> **New score**

### Due and post dates

![Pick the score - Tips](/help/assets/img/activities/post-due-dates.png)

You can fix a **due date** for your assignement to turn in their work.

ðŸ’¡ Students will be still able to submit after the due date but they will be flag as late submissions.

You can also work your assignment when you have free time and **set a date** when they will become available to the classroom.

### Post your assignment

Now that your assignment is ready you can post it. :) 
Here is what student will see from their stream

![student stream](/help/assets/img/activities/stream-student.png)

Once they click on it they will be prompted your instructions to follow

![assignment instructions](/help/assets/img/activities/assignment-description.png)

## 3. Follow up

Now that your assignment are available to your students you can track their progresses.

![Assignment tracking](/help/assets/img/activities/assignment-tracking.png)

The student that appears in the red squared section are those who already started to work on it.
Once they submit something, you will receive a notification
